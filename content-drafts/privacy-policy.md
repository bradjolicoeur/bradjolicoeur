This Privacy Policy describes how bradjolicoeur.com collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this site.

## What Information Is Collected

### Contact Form
When you voluntarily submit the contact form, the following information is collected:
- Your name
- Your email address
- The message you write

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry. It is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing purposes.

### Analytics
This site uses [Umami](https://umami.is/) for privacy-respecting analytics. Umami collects no personal data, uses no cookies, and is fully compliant with GDPR and CCPA out of the box. No consent banner is required because nothing personally identifiable is tracked. Umami measures aggregate metrics only — page views, referrers, and general usage patterns — to help improve the site.

## What Is NOT Collected
- No cookies are set by this site
- No tracking pixels or third-party advertising scripts
- No fingerprinting or device identification
- No personal data beyond what you explicitly submit via the contact form

## Data Retention
Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and are not stored in a public database.

## Third-Party Services
This site uses the following external services:
- **Umami Analytics** — privacy-first, no personal data collected ([umami.is/privacy](https://umami.is/privacy))
- **BlastCMS** — content delivery
- **GitHub / LinkedIn** — outbound links only; their own privacy policies apply

## Your Rights
If you submitted the contact form and would like your information removed, contact me at the address on the [Contact](/contact) page and I will delete it promptly.

## Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
